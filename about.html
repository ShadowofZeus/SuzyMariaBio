About Susan...

As a young girl from a small town of Harare,I have big dreams of being Harare's next top model and chef. Cooking came naturally to me at a very tender age and my height did not stop me from getting to the stove as my mother ended up placing a stool in the kitchen. I enjoy cooking with a passion and experimenting with different menus and fusing delicacies to a well-prepared delectable meal. My family members enjoy my talent when I am at home with my nephews giving me the deserved respect on premise that I serve them first after cooking.

I am also a marketer for an upcoming solar company and strongly believe in the Green Revolution. I am young church leader, and aspiring catwalk model and a loving girlfriend to a computer geek.
